### ✦ SHERLOCK HOLMES I
**1854 – c.1957 — London / Sussex, England** · [DOYLE] [BG] [REN] [WN]

Son of William Scott Holmes. Born 6 January 1854 in Yorkshire. The gift fully expressed: the world's first and only consulting detective. The sixty stories of Arthur Conan Doyle document his active career from 1878 to 1903 in more detail than any other figure in this lineage.

Educated at university — Baring-Gould places him at both Oxford and Cambridge; Rennison has him dropping out of Cambridge and briefly working as an actor with Henry Irving. Established in London as a consulting detective from 1878. Met Dr. John H. Watson in January 1881 at St Bartholomew's Hospital. 221B Baker Street. Mrs Hudson. The violin. The cocaine. Irene Adler. Moriarty. Reichenbach Falls, May 1891 — the apparent death. The Great Hiatus, 1891–1894 — Tibet, Persia, Mecca, the Sudan, Montpellier. The return. The later cases. Retirement to Sussex, 1903, to keep bees and write *Practical Handbook of Bee Culture.* Last documented public service 1914, *His Last Bow* — breaking a German spy ring at the age of sixty. Holmes noted that his ancestors could be traced back to Norman times and that one of his distant relatives married the Commander in Chief of the Naval Forces of His Holiness the Pope. One pastiche records him finally dying on his one hundred and third birthday, with the name "Irene" on his lips.

*Children:*
- **Sherlock Holmes II** — direct line; son by Miss Falkland; see Book IX.
- Raffles Holmes — son by Marjorie Raffles; documented in the Wold Newton family tree.
- Mycroft Adler Norton — elder son by Irene Adler; conceived during *A Scandal in Bohemia* (1888); raised as the son of Godfrey Norton.
- Nero Wolfe — twin son by Irene Adler; born 1892 in Montenegro; the great American detective of 35th Street, New York; chronicled by Rex Stout in 33 novels and 39 novellas; his earlier life as Auguste Lupa documented by John Lescroart in *Son of Holmes* (1986) and *Rasputin's Revenge* (1987).
- Marko Vukcic — twin son by Irene Adler; born 1892 in Montenegro; Nero Wolfe's lifelong friend and restaurateur.
- Shirley Holmes II — daughter by Miss Falkland; her grandson Dan Robinson bore an unmistakeable physical and intellectual resemblance to Sherlock Holmes.
- Minerva Holmes — daughter by Vivian La Graine; no further record.
- Alice "Boomer" La Graine — daughter by Vivian La Graine; no further record.

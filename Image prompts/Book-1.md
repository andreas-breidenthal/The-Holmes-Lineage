### 1. UR-NAMMA-IQ (~2050 BC — Ur, Third Dynasty of Ur)

**Prompt:**

He has a broad, weathered face with prominent cheekbones and a strong jaw, dark brown skin typical of southern Mesopotamia, deep-set black eyes of extraordinary observational sharpness and intelligence, and close-cropped dark hair with grey at the temples. His beard is full but neatly trimmed in the Sumerian style. He wears a white fleeced kaunakes wool garment draped over one shoulder, with a clay cylinder seal on a cord around his neck. He sits at a low assessment table covered in clay tablets, one hand pressing lightly on a cuneiform tablet, the other holding a stylus. His expression is one of precise, concentrated authority — a man who has just seen something in the numbers that no one else has noticed. The lighting is warm oil-lamp amber, casting angled shadows across the clay tablets and stone assessment room of the Nanna ziggurat at Ur. High resolution, museum-quality portrait photography aesthetic, depth of field bringing his face into sharp focus, the tablets softly blurred in the foreground.

# BOOK IV: THE FRANKISH & NORSE WORLD
## *~370 AD – ~1090 AD*
### Gothic Lands · Frankish Territory · Frisia · Vestfold · Yorkshire

---

*All entries in this book are gap-fill composition — original work, historically grounded, consistent with the archaeological and textual record. No canonical or pastiche source documents this period of the lineage. The name transforms dramatically across this book: from Gothic "Hulmar" to Frankish "Holmar" to Old Norse "Holmr" — arriving in English soil as the family that will, in time, become Holmes.*

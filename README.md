# The Holmes Lineage
## *A Complete Biographical Record, ~2050 BC – 2021 AD*

This repository contains a speculative biographical chronicle tracing the direct ancestral line of Sherlock Holmes from the Third Dynasty of Ur to the present day — 132 named individuals across four thousand years, organised into nine books.

The project began from the observation Sherlock Holmes himself made: that his ancestors were country squires who appeared to have little connection to the analytical gift that distinguished him. This chronicle proposes that the gift was not an anomaly but an inheritance — present in every generation, expressed differently in each age, and traceable in an unbroken line from a temple assessor in ancient Mesopotamia to a consulting detective in Victorian London and beyond.
